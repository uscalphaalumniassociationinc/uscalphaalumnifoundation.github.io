Education is the key that unlocks countless possibilities. Yet, for many Filipino children, this key remains out of reach. The USC Alpha Alumni Association Foundation is committed to changing that through Project Alpha. Our scholarship program supports the education of children whose parents are incarcerated and underprivileged youth, giving them the tools to break the cycle of poverty and become the leaders of tomorrow.

Scholarship Program

Program Overview

Project Alpha provides scholarships for secondary education and vocational training to students whose parents are deprived of liberty, and underprivileged youth. Our program covers tuition fees, allowances, books, and school supplies, along with mentoring and leadership development opportunities.

FAQs

General Questions

1. What is Project Alpha?

Project Alpha is a scholarship program initiated by the USC Alpha Alumni Association Foundation. It aims to support the education of children whose parents are incarcerated, providing them with financial assistance, mentoring, and leadership development opportunities.

2. Who can apply for the Project Alpha scholarship?

Students whose parents are currently deprived of liberty and underprivileged youths who meet our academic and financial criteria are eligible to apply for the scholarship.

3. What types of educational programs does the scholarship cover?

The scholarship covers secondary education and vocational training programs.

Application Process

4. How do I apply for the scholarship?

To apply for the scholarship, follow these steps:

  • Check eligibility criteria.
  • Gather the required documents.
  • Complete the application form.
  • Submit your application by the specified deadline.

5. What documents are required for the application?

You will need to provide proof of your parents incarceration, academic records, a recommendation letter, and a personal statement. Additional documents may be required as specified in the application guidelines.

6. When is the application deadline?

The application deadlines vary each year. Please check our website or contact us for the most current information.

Scholarship Benefits

7. What expenses does the scholarship cover?

The scholarship covers tuition fees, monthly allowances, books, school supplies, mentoring, and opportunities for leadership development.

8. How long does the scholarship last?

The scholarship is awarded on an annual basis and can be renewed each year based on the scholars academic performance and adherence to the program guidelines.

9. Can the scholarship be used for expenses other than tuition?

Yes, the scholarship also provides a monthly allowance, books, and school supplies. It also includes mentoring and leadership development opportunities.

Selection Process

10. How are scholars selected?

Scholars are selected based on their academic performance, financial need, personal statement, and recommendations. The selection committee reviews all applications and conducts interviews with shortlisted candidates.

11. How will I be notified if I am selected?

Selected candidates will be notified via email and phone. Announcements will also be made on our website and social media channels.

Donations and Volunteering

12. How can I donate to Project Alpha?

You can donate through our website using our secure online donation form. We accept one-time and recurring donations. For more information, please visit our "Get Involved" section.

13. Can I volunteer for Project Alpha?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Whether you are an educator, mentor, or community organizer, your involvement can make a significant impact. Please visit our "Get Involved" section to learn more about volunteer opportunities.

14. How can I get more information about Project Alpha?

For more information, please visit our website or contact us via email at info@uscalpha.org

15. Are there any events or workshops conducted by Project Alpha?

Yes, we regularly conduct events and workshops for our scholars. Please check our Blog or Events page for updates on upcoming activities.

16. How can I stay updated on Project Alpha’s activities and news?

You can stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter, following us on social media, and regularly visiting our Blog page.
