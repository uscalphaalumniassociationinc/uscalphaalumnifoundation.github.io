The Project Alpha

USC Alpha Alumni Foundation

Igniting Hope, Building Futures

Education is the key that unlocks countless possibilities. Yet, for many Filipino children, this key remains out of reach. The USC Alpha Alumni Association Foundation is committed to changing that.

Our nation's youth are its greatest asset. They are the leaders, innovators, and change-makers of tomorrow. However, systemic challenges, such as poverty and family incarceration, often hinder their potential.

Project Alpha is our answer to this pressing issue. We offer scholarships for secondary education and vocational training to deserving students whose parents are currently deprived of liberty. By investing in these young minds, we are not only providing financial support but also nurturing hope, resilience, and a strong sense of purpose.

Why Project Alpha Matters:

  • Breaking the cycle of poverty: Education is a powerful tool for lifting families out of poverty and creating a brighter future for generations to come.
  • Empowering young leaders: Our scholars will become catalysts for positive change in their communities, inspiring others and contributing to nation-building.
  • Restoring hope: By supporting these students, we offer a beacon of hope in challenging circumstances, demonstrating that society cares about their well-being.

Scholarship Benefits:

  • Tuition Fees: Full coverage of tuition fees to ensure financial barriers do not hinder academic pursuits.
  • Allowance: Monthly stipends to cover basic needs and school-related expenses.
  • Books and School Supplies: Provision of essential learning materials.
  • Mentoring and Guidance: Access to mentors who provide academic support and career guidance.
  • Opportunities for Leadership Development: Programs and workshops to develop leadership skills and community involvement.
